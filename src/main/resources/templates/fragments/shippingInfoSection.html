Shipping Information

Fast and Reliable Shipping to Your Doorstep

Domestic Shipping:

Standard Shipping: Orders are processed within 1-2 business days and delivered within 5-7 business days. Shipping is free for orders over $50.
Express Shipping: Need it faster? Choose our express shipping option to receive your order within 2-3 business days. Additional charges apply.
Overnight Shipping: For urgent needs, we offer overnight shipping to ensure your package arrives the next business day. Please place your order before 12 PM for same-day processing.

International Shipping:

Standard International Shipping: Delivery times vary by destination, typically taking 10-20 business days. Import duties and taxes may apply.
Expedited International Shipping: Faster delivery options are available for an additional fee, with typical delivery times of 5-10 business days.

Additional Information:

Order Tracking: Once your order is shipped, you will receive a confirmation email with tracking information. You can monitor the status of your shipment in real-time.
Shipping Costs: Shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on the weight and destination of your order.
Packaging: We take great care in packaging your items to ensure they arrive in perfect condition. If your order arrives damaged, please contact our customer service team within 48 hours.
Returns and Exchanges: If you are not satisfied with your purchase, we offer a hassle-free return and exchange policy. Please refer to our Returns and Exchanges page for more details.
