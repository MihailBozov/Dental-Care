Returns & Exchanges Information

Hassle-Free Returns and Easy Exchanges

Return Policy

Time Frame: You may return items within 30 days of delivery for a full refund or exchange.
Condition: Items must be unused, in the same condition as received, and in the original packaging.
Exceptions: Some items, such as personalized or custom-made products, may not be eligible for return. Please check the product description for details.

Exchange Process

How to Exchange: To exchange an item, simply contact our customer service team to initiate the exchange process.
Exchange Options: You can exchange your item for a different size, color, or product of equal value. If the new item costs more, you will be responsible for paying the price difference.

Additional Information

Damaged or Defective Items: If you receive a damaged or defective item, please contact us immediately.
Return Shipping: While we do not cover the cost of return shipping for standard returns, we may provide a prepaid return label for defective or incorrect items.
Refund Status: Once your refund is processed, you will receive an email notification. Please allow 3-5 business days for the refund to reflect on your bank or credit card statement.
